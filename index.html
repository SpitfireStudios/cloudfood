How does it work?

Simple.

We provide a location, a kitchen, personnel and delivery services.

You provide your chef to ensure quality and dramatically extend your service range.

Why the concept?

We believe in shared spaces.

There are plenty of things we can learn from one another so another major interest for us is fostering collaborative spaces.

It’s not a necessity so you can maintain the privacy of your station but we encourage working together, who knows what your chef may learn?

What does this mean for you?

In laymen’s terms as you can imagine this means an enormous reduction in costs.

You don’t need to spend ridiculous amounts maintaining a building, hiring servers and tending to customers’ needs constantly.

It’s as close to pure profit as you’re going to get.
